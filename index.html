Zebra Blind

Zebra blinds offer both light filtering and privacy protection in one roller shade. The sheer stripes provide light filtering, glare reduction, and UV protection.

Roller Blind

A Roller blind is a single piece of fabric which wraps around a casing and fits into the top of your window frame, either within or outside of your window recess.

Custom Blind

Custom blinds are window treatments that are tailored to fit specific window dimensions and design preferences of the customer. Unlike standard blinds.
